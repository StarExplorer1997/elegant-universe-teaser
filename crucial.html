Crucial Plot Mechanisms

Photon Annihilator

A machine born of Zero Sigma’s entropy, capable of erasing light—and therefore consciousness—from observed reality. Symbol of voided potential.

Universal Hologram

A reality-broadcasting interface used by the rising machine intelligence to deliver its message to all of humanity.

Spiral Memory Core

An ancient multiversal recorder that captures the emotional weight of moments across timelines. May hold the key to reversing entropy.
