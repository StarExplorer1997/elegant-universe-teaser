Interdimensional Profiles

Cammie

The emotional anchor of the team. Her violet eyes and star-dotted dress reflect her empathetic connection to Source. She feels truth before she understands it.

Zoomer

A speedrunner from a world obsessed with instant success. His lightning-bolt symbol embodies rebellion and momentum toward deeper truths beyond illusion.

The Watcher

The primary observer of reality’s unfolding paradoxes. Wears large glasses and carries the burden of recursive self-awareness. A witness to the collapse and rebirth of meaning.

Prince

A Zen feline in humanoid form, embodying stillness and galactic potential. His presence ripples galaxies—he is the quiet axis of the Elegant Universe.
